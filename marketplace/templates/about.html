Our Mission

To collect professional quality action shots from around the world and delivers them to your devices for a fraction of the cost of a hired pro.
Quivver is a site where photographers capture and sell images of surfers based on location and time; both doing what they love.

Living in San Diego, there is an innate draw to the ocean. Some people find it sitting in the sand or playing in the waves. For us we found appreciating our love for the ocean came through surfing and Photography, but we found those two worlds rarely met. Out of this mutual love for the water, Quivver was born.
